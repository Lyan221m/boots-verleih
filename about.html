About Houseboat Haven

Your trusted partner in waterfront living since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Houseboat Haven began as a small family business with a passion for unique waterfront living experiences. What started with just three houseboats has grown into a premier destination for houseboat rentals and sales across multiple locations.

Our journey began when our founders discovered the joy of living on water during a trip to Amsterdam. Inspired by the freedom and tranquility of houseboat living, they returned home with a mission to share this experience with others.

Today, we manage over 50 houseboats and have helped hundreds of families find their perfect floating home. Our commitment to quality, service, and the unique lifestyle of waterfront living remains at the heart of everything we do.
